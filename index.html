From MVP to Startup

PennApps Accelerator is an eight week program that offers 5 fledgling startups access to beta testers, industry mentorship, and investors in the Philadelphia area. At the end of the program, our teams will have a chance to pitch their product to Philly angel investors. We will be accepting applications from teams of up to four founders for our Spring 2014 session until March 15th.

The Accelerator Advantage

Entrepreneurship doesn't happen in a vacuum. The best startups are supported every step of the way. PennApps Accelerator provides your team with all of the following:

  • Weekly work meetups to guage progress
  • Entrepreneurship workshops and brainstorming sessions
  • Hacking space
  • Volunteer beta testers
  • Network of contacts in the tech industry
  • Active community of other entrepreneurial students
  • $1000 grant (we take no equity)
